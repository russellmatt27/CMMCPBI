Data Protection Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines and procedures for the protection and handling of sensitive and confidential data to ensure compliance with regulatory standards and protect the organization’s information assets.

Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who have access to the organization’s information systems and data.

Policy Details

  • Access Control: Access to sensitive data shall be restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Data Encryption: All sensitive data shall be encrypted in transit and at rest using approved encryption standards.
  • Data Retention: Data shall be retained only as long as necessary to fulfill business and regulatory requirements.
  • Incident Response: Any suspected or confirmed data breach shall be reported immediately and managed according to the incident response plan.

Responsibilities

All employees are responsible for adhering to this policy and taking appropriate measures to protect sensitive data. Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action.

Review

This policy shall be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure continued effectiveness and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
